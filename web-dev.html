Web Development is a branch within software engineering that is primarily dedicated to devloping websites that are hosted via internet.

Web Development is broadly broken down into three main components, which are Front-End, Back-End, and Version Control.

Therefore, a typical tech company roles could be categorized into Front-End developer, Back-end developer, etc.

However, many companies also aim to hire Full-Stack developers, who are able to do all the jobs of Front-End, as well as, Back-End developers.
